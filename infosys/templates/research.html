Proposed research :Finding methods to improve English in primary school students in Alakolawewa School-Walapane education zone


1. Research hypothesis: Computer assisted reading in englsih imporove test scores
2. Null hypothesis: There's no difference in test scores between the group who read with computers and the group with normal reading with a teacher
3. Methodology:
Sampling procedure : Assign grade 4 and grade 3 students to two groups randomly and give one group of students instruction in englsih with assiatance of computer software with functions of immediate text to speech and translation of english to sinhala
Procedure: Give Instructions to read the same lessons in grade 3 and grade 4 text books to experimental group having assisted computers and no teacher and control group with teacher assistance only
Duration : 15 School Days
Administer a standardized reading test to all students. Match students in computer assisted reading classes with students in regular teacher class.
Measurements:Total correctly spoken words percentage,time to finish the test and total correctly translated words percenatge .Calculate the mean of two groups after giving post test for above 3 diffent measurments.
Data Analysis:Test the null hypothesis by using a statistical test to determine if the difference in the means could easily be a function of chance alone.
Further Research:
Test the hypotheis whether both teacher and computer assisted reading would yield better results than reading with teacher and reading with computer by dividing students into 3 groups.
